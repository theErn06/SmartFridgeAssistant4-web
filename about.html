Smart Refrigerator Assistant

(Project Jarvis)

Developed by Year 3 students from the Bachelor of Electrical & Electronics (E&E) program at TARUMT.

This system integrates advanced Edge-LLM technology, real-time voice recognition, and custom hardware to create a seamless, voice-activated smart kitchen experience.
