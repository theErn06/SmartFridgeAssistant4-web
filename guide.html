User Guide

System Overview

Welcome to the Jarvis Smart Fridge dashboard. This application synchronizes directly with your local smart refrigerator hardware, allowing you to monitor your groceries and track food waste from anywhere.

Hardware & Arduino Guide

  • üîå

    LCD Display Panel

    The Arduino Uno is connected to an LCD display that provides real-time feedback. It shows "Listening..." when waiting for commands, "Recording..." when you speak, and prints Jarvis's text responses directly on the screen.

  • üö™

    Smart Door Sensor

    A physical sensor tracks when the fridge door is opened or closed. If the door is left open for more than 30 seconds, the hardware triggers a watchdog reminder and Jarvis will audibly warn you to close the fridge to save energy and prevent food spoilage.
