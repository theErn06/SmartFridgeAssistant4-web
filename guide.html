User Guide

System Overview

Welcome to the Jarvis Smart Fridge dashboard. This web application synchronizes directly with your local smart refrigerator hardware, allowing you to monitor your groceries and track food waste from anywhere using your cloud account.

    Voice Mode (Speak to PC)

    You can directly speak to your PC's microphone to talk to Jarvis! Simply say "Hey Jarvis" (or your configured wake word) followed by a natural command like "Add two apples" or "Remove the milk." The system uses advanced AI STT to instantly transcribe and process your speech.

    Live Inventory

    Navigate to the Inventory tab to view your current fridge stock in real-time. The table displays item quantities, categorized groupings, and exact expiration dates. Items that have passed their expiration will display a bold red warning in the "Days Left" column.
